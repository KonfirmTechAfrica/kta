KONFIRM TECH AFRICA Internship Program

Konfirm is striving to reinvent learning, nurture talent and inspire people to make lifelong positive career decisions as well as carrying out Corporate Social Responsibilities by training and mentoring talent within our communities.

Program Benefits

Mentorship from Industry Experts

Gain valuable insights and guidance from industry-leading AI and Data Science professionals who will provide mentorship during the program.

Hands-on Projects

Apply your learning to real-world projects, providing you with practical experience and a strong portfolio.

Career Advancement

Prepare for a successful career in AI and Data Science with our comprehensive program, opening doors to exciting opportunities.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with a diverse and supportive community of fellow learners and professionals in the field.

Access to Tools

Explore the latest AI and Data Science tools and technologies, including generative AI frameworks, to stay ahead of the curve.



Program Structure

To ensure inclusivity and accessibility, we’ve created a structured program with three levels, and integrated generative AI concepts into each level. Konfirm Tech Africa helps you gain real world working experience within 3 - 6 months. Real Projects, Expert Mentors, Fully Remote and Flexible!

Available Tech Paths

Our program focuses on participants with prior experience either by going through courses, bootcamps, or self learning. You must have prior coding or design skills/knowledge!

↓

Frontend Developer

The frontend path will focus on using any framework to build high quality web applications. You should already have basic skills and the computer to develop web applications. You must know one framework prior to joining thi track.

javascript | ReactJs | NextJs| Angular | VueJs

Product Management

The product management path is focused on people who want to learn how to manage teams and deliver products. Each product manager will deal with a relatively large team, and will be responsible for bringing the team to success.

Excel | Notion | Linear

Backend Development

The backend path will focus on using any framework to build high quality backend API. You should already have basic skills and the computer to develop web applications. You must know one framework prior to joining this track.

Python | NodeJS | PHP | Go | C#

Virtual Assistant

The virtual assistant path is designed for those who want to support businesses by managing administrative tasks, organizing schedules, and handling communication. Each virtual assistant will work closely with clients to ensure smooth day-to-day operations and improve productivity.

Google Workspace | Trello | Slack | AI

Product Design (UI/UX)

The product design (U/UX) path is for people showing lots of promise in design. We challenge the participants to create beautiful designs for web and mobile applications. We use Figma for design.

Figma | Framer | Webflow

Data Analytics

The data analytics path is for individuals who want to learn how to analyze data, generate insights, and help businesses make informed decisions. Each data analyst will work with large datasets and will be responsible for turning raw data into actionable strategies.

Excel | SQL | Tableau

Frequently Asked Question?

Who can attend the internship? +
While beginners can try, it's ideal for those with some coding design, or product management experience. Apply during the open registration period.
Will I be able to juggle my daytime job as well? +
Our program is highly flexible.
Can I get a job following the internship? +
Judging by our curriculum, the skills you acquire at the advanced stage of the program may be just the right amount needed to secure a job. However, this is highly dependent on you as an individual.
Can I switch from one learning path to another after the program has commenced? +
No. Migration from one path to another after the program has started is not allowed.
is the Internship onSite or onLine? +
The program is mostly remote for most participants therefore, we are taking applications from participants resident in Africa.
